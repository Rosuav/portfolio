About Me

Joseph Berthiaume has been working in the IT field for over 16 years. Holding a degree in electrical engineering, he has extensive experience in all levels of system administration and network engineering.

Bulding on that experience, along with many hours spent working with MS SQL, he has now moved fully into modern software engineering for the web. His focus is currently in front end development with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Javascript and AJAX technologies with plans to go full stack in the immediate future.

Joseph lives in NYC with his beautiful wife, new baby boy, dozens of fish and corals, and his loyal coding assistant Penny the Golden Retriever.

Contact Joseph today to inquire about hiring him for a job, big or small.
