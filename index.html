About Me

I started my career with a degree in Electrical Engineering and quickly moved into the IT field where I spent over 16 years gaining experience as a Network and System Engineer. I have managed virtually every aspect of operations from desktop support to server configuration and administration to business continuity and disaster recovery.

Building on that experience, along with many hours spent working with MS SQL, I have now moved fully into modern software engineering for the web. My focus is currently in Front End Development with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Javascript and AJAX technologies and plan to continue the never ending learning process by expanding into the full stack with server side technologies. I enjoy collaborating with others on all sorts of projects and am always on the look out for the next challenge.

I live in NYC with my beautiful wife, our baby boy, dozens of fish and corals, and my loyal coding assistant Penny the Golden Retriever.

Please contact to me today to inquire about hiring me for a job, big or small, or just talk about the latest in technology.

The data is returned and displayed using a Masonry gallery below some introductory Wikipedia data about the desired reef fish.
